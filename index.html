About Us

We are a student-led club that aims to make STEM education accessible to students from various backgrounds. Our goal is to spread computer literacy to underprivileged schools in Nepal. We want to make knowledge more accessible for students in a sustainable manner.

As students of the IB Diploma Programme at Ullens, this club was created as a part of our CAS. All of our founding members have our own interests regarding computer science, but we had one goal in common; from our own experiences as well as general societal issues found here in Nepal (specifically Kathmandu) we have seen that literacy in the technological field is gathering more importance everyday. Technology is rapidly becoming a huge part of our day to day lives, and it is increasingly important for people to be aware of how to navigate this as well as understand how it affects them without getting lost. This observation has prompted us to realise the importance of providing knowledge on STEM, therefore feuling us to create this club.

By hosting workshops at various schools as well as by inviting guest speakers for seminars, our purpose is to provide knowledge on various STEM topics.
